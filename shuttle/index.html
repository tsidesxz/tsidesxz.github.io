About Service

Shuttle ticket booking is available at Easiway App. We provied bus and van to cross border from Hong Kong to Shezhen or vise versa. Easiway runs 4 stops total from Shenzhen and link to 6 lines in Hong Kong.

How it works

  • Van customer departing from SZ Bao'an Airport will ride a bus to Futian Bus Terminal and transfer to a cross-border van traveling to Hong Kong Airport.

  • Van customer departing from SZ North Station will ride a regular van to Futian Bus Terminal and transfer to a cross-border van travelling to Hong Kong Airport.

  • Van customer departing from SZ Bao'an Airport will ride a bus to Futian Bus Terminal and transfer to a cross-border van traveling to Hong Kong Airport.

  • Van customer departing from SZ North Station will ride a regular van to Futian Bus Terminal and transfer to a cross-border van travelling to Hong Kong Airport.

  • App shows where to get your tickets printed and board the ride.

Visa

Valid Chinese visa is required to cross border from Hong Kong to Shenzhen or vise versa. So please make sure and check your Chinse / Hong Kong visa or permission is vaild. If you are foreigner and don't have Chinese visa, driver will take you to Huangguang port where you can get arrival visa.
